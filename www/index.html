How To Use
Tile Calc is designed to calculate the tiles, adhesive and grout you require for a given area.

  1. Input the Width, Length & Depth of the tile you are using and the Grout Width you require in millimetres.
  2. Select the extra for cutting and wastage. The default is 10%.
  3. Enter the Area to tile in square metres.

Click Calculate to see the recommended quantities for tiles, adhesive and grout.
Use this as a guide. Please check with your supplier before purchasing.
All measurements are metric.
